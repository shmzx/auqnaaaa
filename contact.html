History

The website is dedicated to Basic Life Support (BLS) and the transformative journey of two nursing students, Masangya and Pioquid, who came together to create a platform that provides accessible and comprehensive information about BLS. They studied the history of BLS, including pioneers like Dr. Peter Safar and Dr. James Elam, and organizations like the American Heart Association and ILCOR. They curate valuable resources, educational materials, and practical tips for individuals seeking to learn and enhance their BLS skills. The website aims to bridge the gap between theory and practice, providing clear information on BLS techniques. The students also provide guidance and support for fellow nursing students on their BLS journey. They believe that equipping individuals with BLS knowledge can collectively improve emergency outcomes and save lives.
